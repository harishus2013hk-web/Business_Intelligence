# Footwear Sales Performance Dashboard

An interactive **Footwear Sales Analytics Dashboard** built to monitor and analyze key business metrics including sales, profit, margin, regional performance, product trends, and retailer insights.

## Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of retail business performance across multiple dimensions such as:

* **Sales & Profit Tracking**
* **Operating Margin Analysis**
* **Units Sold Monitoring**
* **Regional Performance Comparison**
* **Retailer Performance Insights**
* **Product Category Analysis**
* **Growth Trends Over Time**

## Key Features

### Filters / Slicers

* Date Range Selector
* Region Filter
* City Filter
* Retailer Filter
* Product Filter

### KPI Cards

* Total Units Sold
* Operating Profit
* Total Sales
* Operating Margin
* Growth Percentage

### Visualizations Included

* Sales vs Operating Profit by Product
* Units Sold by Retailer & Sales Method
* Top 10 Cities by Operating Profit
* Units Sold by Region (Donut Chart)
* Sales by Region & Sales Method
* Top Retailers (Sales & Growth)
* Monthly Growth Trend Analysis
* Yearly & Quarterly Operating Margin/Profit Trend
* Detailed Transaction Table

## Insights Generated

* Identify top-performing retailers and cities
* Track regional sales contribution
* Compare online vs outlet vs in-store performance
* Analyze product-wise profitability
* Monitor seasonal/monthly growth patterns
